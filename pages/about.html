About HealthFlow AI

Transforming healthcare management with AI-powered automation

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare delivery across Africa by automating administrative burden and enabling healthcare professionals to focus on patient care.

We believe that healthcare technology should be accessible, affordable, and impactful. HealthFlow AI is built specifically for resource-limited healthcare settings in Western Uganda and beyond.

Empowering healthcare teams to save 12-15 hours weekly and transform patient outcomes.
