About HealthFlow AI

Transforming healthcare through intelligent automation

Our Mission

We're on a mission to eliminate the administrative burden that keeps healthcare workers from doing what they do best: caring for patients.

Healthcare facilities in Western Uganda face overwhelming workloads managing HIV, chronic disease, maternal health, and medication adherence. Our platform automates these processes, freeing up 12-15 hours per week so healthcare teams can focus on patient care.
